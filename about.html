ABOUT ME

Oh hey, you're still here? Must be the ASCII face that piqued your curiosity. I'm a UI/UX designer with a passion for creating designs that don't just look beautiful — they work beautifully too.


I've always been that person who can't help but analyze every design I come across. I may not be a perfect designer, but I've certainly mastered the art of curiosity. I love diving into the why behind each design decision so it helps me during my own design process. During my first internship as a UI/UX Designer using Figma, the little snippets of code on the dev side sparked an interest in Frontend. Let's just say I still prefer perfecting pixels to debugging them.


Whether it's crafting intuitive interfaces or fine-tuning the code behind them, I thrive at the intersection of design and development, making me a true all-rounder in the digital space.
